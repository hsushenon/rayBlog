This is the strange one that I got interested in the more I started to understand it. I have watched a few matches live, and it was entertaining. **Baseball**

When I went to Canada, my brother and I went to see the Toronto Blue Jays play the Houston Astros. It was a fun experience. Since I started to follow the Dodgers because of Shohei Ohtani, I recently watched the World Series. They were great games. Especially the final game 7. I have not watched a game/match so intensely for a long time now, unless it's an Arsenal match. Shohei Ohtani is probably my favourite sports star now across all sports.
